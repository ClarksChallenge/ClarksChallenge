Privacy Policy

Last updated: TODAY

1. Who we are

fundup is a simple fundraising tool that allows supporters to complete surveys to generate money for creators. We are designed to operate with minimal data and maximum privacy.

2. What we do not collect

When you use fundup, we do not ask for or store:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Passwords or accounts
  • Payment card details
  • Demographic information (age, gender, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers

3. Anonymous creator IDs

When you visit a creator’s page, the URL includes an anonymous creator ID (for example: ?id=CREATOR_ABC). This ID is used only to route survey earnings to the correct creator. It does not identify a person.

4. Surveys and third‑party providers

Surveys on fundup are provided by CPX Research, a third‑party survey provider. When you interact with surveys, you are interacting directly with CPX Research, which may collect limited information needed to run surveys and prevent fraud.

CPX Research is responsible for its own data practices and complies with privacy regulations such as GDPR and CCPA.

5. Cookies and tracking

fundup itself does not set cookies or use tracking pixels. CPX Research may use cookies or similar technologies within their survey iframe to operate their service and prevent abuse.

6. Payouts to creators

Payouts to creators are tracked using anonymous creator IDs and payout amounts. We do not publish or store personal payment details on this site.

7. Data security

Because we intentionally avoid collecting personal data, the risk of data exposure is significantly reduced. We still recommend that creators and supporters avoid sharing personal information in any free‑text fields or communications related to fundup.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact the fundup maintainer via the project’s GitHub repository.
