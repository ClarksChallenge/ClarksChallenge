Payout Transparency

8Impact tracks creator payouts using anonymous creator IDs only. No names, emails, or personal information are ever stored.

Every payout made to a creator is logged in a public, read‑only ledger so creators can verify their balances at any time.

Payout Verification

To request a payout, creators must provide:

  • Their creator ID
  • Their payout passphrase
  • Their preferred payout method (PayPal, Cash App, etc.)

⚠️ 8Impact cannot recover lost passphrases. If a creator loses their passphrase, their earnings cannot be claimed.

This system keeps payouts transparent while protecting everyone’s privacy.
